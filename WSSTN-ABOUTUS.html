"WSSTN: WE SUSTAIN THE EARTH AGAIN" is an initiative approach driven by a youth called by Manuel Rafael. WSSTN is a website that helps us to improve our planet to be better since our planet is endangered of climate change that caused a lot of problems around the world lately. But, with the appearance of this website, I wanted to tell people how can they live in a better way starts with building the basic habits that can be helpful in the long run for the environment. So, with all of these I would like to present to you: "WSSTN: WE SUSTAIN THE EARTH AGAIN."
