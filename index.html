An initiative approach by a youth called by Manuel Rafael

wsstn.

START-UP, the first thing that comes up to my mind when hearing this word is an opportunity. What I meant by the word opportunity is when you can open a new way for a person to open a new page of living. About a few days ago I got an idea to make a start-up company that has a link to my interest, which is programming on front-end development and environmental studies. The start-up company will be called by 'WSSTN' with a slogan of 'We Sustain Our Earth Again.' The Purpose of this start-up is that I wanted people to be more aware of the climate change issue, which is a huge topic to talk now. With all the purpose, this website aims to give information about how we can be more sustainable in living, what other causes that can increase climate change issue with some tips. All of this stuff will be coming up soon with the website launch and social media. So, I hope this company can bring a huge impact to the community to "Sustain Our Earth Again"

- Manuel Rafael -
Founder & CEO of wsstn.
